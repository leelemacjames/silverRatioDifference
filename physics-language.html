IB DP Physics — Critical Language Analysis

The Language
of Physics

When we say heat "flows", electrons "want", or entropy "always" increases — what are we actually claiming?

⬡ Mechanistic ◈ Cartwright ◇ Wittgenstein

Mechanistic

What the model actually says. The formal mechanism, the equation, the conditions under which the statement holds.

Cartwright

Laws are domain-bound. They hold within models, under specific conditions — not as universal truths about an unruly world.

Wittgenstein

Meaning lives in use. Words like "flows", "wants", or "seeks" carry hidden metaphors that shape — and distort — understanding.
